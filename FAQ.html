Frequently Asked Questions
Smartloop Supercharge your sales with Messenger Marketing.
What is a subscriber?
Anytime someone sends a message to your messenger bot, he/ she becomes a subscriber—smartloop pricing based on the number of subscribers. As the number of subscribers grows, the bot automatically upgraded to the next plan and reflected in the next billing cycle.
What channels do you support?
We support Facebook Messenger, web, and Viber. However, using the API, you can build a conversational bot using the Smartloop builder and publish it to other channels. You can use the webhook approach to capture and relay to your channel.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. If you’d like us to accept other forms of payments, please write to us at billing@smartloop.ai for assistance.
Smartloop is a free platform, and we recommend to find your use-case first then put some effort into building out that perfect conversation flow that works for your product/service. The best way is to analyze your conversation flow and keep improving it continuously.
If you have any questions about billing and other general issues, please contact us: admin@smartloop.ai

Can I cancel the premium plan?
Yes, you can cancel your premium plan anytime from bot settings. However, once you have downgraded, you won’t be able to access the premium features anymore.
I need more subscribers
If you need more subscribers from what is in the pricing sheet (please type “pricing” in the web bot to get started), please send us an email at billing@smartloop.ai. We have pricing plans and SLAs designed to support SME’s and large enterprises.
Are there any setup fees?
There are no setup fees and hidden charges with any of our plans. Smartloop is a free platform and anyone can sign up to get started. However, we are here to help and please drop us a line at hello@smartloop.ai
Is my data safe?
We take privacy very seriously at Smartloop. We ensure that your data at rest and in transit are handled securely. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for further information.
Can I integrate with other services?
The pricing is calculated based on number of subscribers of your bot. Once the trial period is over, you will not be able to access the premium features. Your bot will still work as it is, however you wont be able to reach out beyond (broadcasts/audience/campaign) the allowed free plan limitation.
Can I upgrade/downgrade anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade easily anytime based on your needs. Click on the “Configure” tab and scroll to the payments widget.
Do you offer a white-label solution?
Smartloop is a self-service SaaS platform. We do not offer custom white-label solution.
